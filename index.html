FREQUENT QUESTION ANSWERS

SOLVE ALL YOUR PROBLEM ABOUT OUR SERVICES

1. What payment methods do you accept?
We accept all major credit cards, PayPal, bank transfers, and cryptocurrency payments. We also offer flexible payment plans for larger projects to make our services more accessible.
2. Do you offer free design consultations?
Yes, we offer a complimentary initial consultation to understand your vision, space requirements, and budget. This helps us create a customized design proposal for your project.
3. How long does a typical project take?
Project timelines vary depending on scope and complexity. A single room typically takes 4-6 weeks, while full home renovations can take 3-6 months. We provide detailed timelines during planning.
4. Do you provide 3D design previews?
Absolutely! We create detailed 3D renderings and virtual walkthroughs so you can visualize your space before any work begins. This ensures you're completely satisfied with the design.
5. Do you offer installation and project management services?
Yes, we provide complete project management and installation services. Our certified team handles everything from furniture assembly to final styling, ensuring a seamless experience.
